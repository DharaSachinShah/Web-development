Pathaan

'Pathaan' crosses ₹400 cr mark, to surpass SRK's highest-grosser Chennai Express. Based on the Pathan Day 6 Box Office Collection, it is thought that it will break new records over the weekend. Pathan brought in Rs. 132 crores in just two days. And if Pathan keeps getting more popular, it will join the club of movies that have made Pathan Day 6 Box Office Collection more than 600 crore.

Two rockstars in one frame

Kapil Sharma to make singing debut with Guru Randhawa. The two will be releasing a single titled “Alone”. This exciting news has already been praised by singer Mika Singh who has referred to the collaboration as “two rock stars in one frame”.

Vicky Kaushal
